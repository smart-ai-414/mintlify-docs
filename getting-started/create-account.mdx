## Step 1: Select your profile

Pick the option that matches how you'll use The Agency Engineer. We use this to customise your workspace and the tips you see during onboarding.

- **I'm an agency** — Manage multiple client accounts with centralised oversight. Unlocks the multi-client dashboard, team collaboration, and white-label reports.
- **I'm a business** — Run your own marketing and lead-gen campaigns at scale. Unlocks a single-account focus, deep funnel insights, and revenue optimisation.

<Tip>
  Already have an account? Use the **Sign in** link in the top-right corner instead of completing the wizard.
</Tip>

## Step 2: Create your account

This step captures the essentials: your login plus the agency's workspace identity.

### Choose a sign-in method

Pick one of the following to authenticate. You can add the others later from account settings.

- **Continue with Google** — Use your Google Workspace or Gmail identity.
- **Continue with Facebook** — Sign in with your Facebook account.
- **Email and password** — Enter a work email and a password to create a standalone login.

### Your details

Provide a few fields about you:

| Field | Required | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Work email | Yes | Used for login and account recovery. |
| Password | Yes | Required when not using a social provider. |
| Full name | No | Shown to teammates and clients. |
| Phone | No | Optional, used only for account recovery. |

### Your agency

The workspace identity is what your team and clients will see.

| Field | Required | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Agency name | Yes | Displayed across the workspace and in reports. |
| Workspace subdomain | No | We suggest one based on your agency name. You can customise it. |
| Website | No | Optional link shown on shared client surfaces. |
| Country | No | Used for currency and tax defaults. |

<Warning>
  Avoid signing up with a shared inbox or distribution list. If nobody owns the mailbox, account recovery becomes much harder later.
</Warning>
